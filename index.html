Creatures from Kong

Kong

Kong is a gigantic monster gorilla orKaiju who lives on a mysterious island named "Skull Island". It is there where the inhabitants are known to worship him, as he also provides protection from other kaiju.

Godzilla

Godzilla is a giant radioactive charged reptile who can live both underwater and also on land. Godzilla is one of the most powerful kaiju of them all.

Mothra

Mothra is a giant powerful moth, who lives in a sleep state. It is all poweful and protects the people who worship it. It is known to be the most powerful beast, while also being able to calm Godzilla.
